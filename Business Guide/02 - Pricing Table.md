## Price Adjustments

### Charge MORE (+%) when:
- Urgent deadline (under 4 weeks) → add 30%
- Client provides no content → add R5,000
- Scope is unclear or keeps changing → charge hourly
- Travel required → add travel costs
- After-hours emergency → double rate

### Charge LESS (-%) when:
- First 2-3 portfolio clients → reduce by 30-40%
- Client signs 12-month retainer upfront → 10% discount
- Client refers another paying client → 10% discount on next invoice

---

## Payment Terms (Non-Negotiable)

**Development projects:**
- 50% deposit before work starts
- 25% on prototype delivery
- 25% on final delivery and go-live

**Monthly retainer:**
- Due on the 1st of each month
- 7 days grace period
- 10% late fee after 7 days
- Services suspended after 30 days overdue

**Never start work without a deposit.**
**Never go live without full payment.**
